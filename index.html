Product and Services

LabGenius : Hematology

Experience the power of customization with LabGenius: Hematology. Our flagship software effortlessly integrates with any laboratory workflow, adapting to the unique needs of every brand and manufacturer of hematology machines.

Equipped with advanced clinical data management features, LabGenius: Hematology seamlessly transfers vital patient data from the hematology machine to a med tech's workstation. Say goodbye to manual collation – our software effortlessly organizes the data per patient, allowing for easy report generation and convenient printing or email distribution. Streamline your processes and elevate your laboratory efficiency with LabGenius: Hematology.

  • Secure and automatic transfer of data from machine to workstation
  • Easy patient profile set up
  • Automatic assignment of processed sample to patientset
  • Fully customizable reports and normal values
  • Easy report generation in pdf format
